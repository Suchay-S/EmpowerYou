Mental Health Challenges

Challenges to Improve Mental Health

  • Gratitude Journal: Write down three things you're grateful for each day(best in the morning!).
  • Mindful Breathing: Practice deep breathing exercises for relaxation.
  • Social Connection: Reach out to a friend or family member for a meaningful conversation.
  • Physical Activity: Be active for atleast 1 hour everyday and reap the benefits.
  • Healthy Diet: Eat balanced meals with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Cut out proccesed foods when possible.
  • Tech Break: Take a break from screens and digital devices for a day, or as long as possible.
  • Practice Mindfulness: Focus on the present moment and let go of worries.
  • Creative Outlet: Spend time on a hobby or creative activity, such as drawing.
  • Positive Affirmations: Repeat affirming statements to boost self-esteem.
  • Seek Professional Help: It's okay to get help, so feel free to reach out to a proffesional therapist or counselor.
